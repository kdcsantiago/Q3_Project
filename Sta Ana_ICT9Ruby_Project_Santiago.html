What is a fire prevention website for?

A fire prevention website is a website that provides information on how to avoid fires. It also explains how to keep your home safe, how to prepare for fires, and what to do if there is a fire. It may also provide the most recent information on fires and how to prevent them.

What causes fires indoors?

Fires can happen indoors if someone forgot to turn off the stove while cooking, or because too many things were plugged into an electrical outlet. Other common causes include things like smoking indoors or not storing flammable materials safely.
